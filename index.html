Features & Info
Lock Screen Controls
While playing, your phone shows the book title with previous, play, and next controls. Works on Android and desktop Chrome automatically.
Install as App
Use the Install button to add Legible to your home screen — no app store needed. On iPhone: Safari → Share → Add to Home Screen.
Home Screen Widgets
Native home screen widgets require an App Store app. This is a web app and cannot create those, but works great as a standalone installed app.
Books Stay Saved
All PDFs and reading positions are saved in your browser. Close the tab, come back — everything is right here. Delete books anytime.

Remove book?

This removes the book and your reading position. It cannot be undone.
